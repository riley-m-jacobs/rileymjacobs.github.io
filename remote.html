About

Since the beginning of the fall 2020, Ms. Jacobs applied analysis methods for CSR GRACE and GRACE-FO data while simultaneously uploading into Google Earth Engine. Over time, Ms. Jacobs has created three sets of GRACE and GRACE-FO data -- GRACE Mascon RL06 solutions, land mask solutions and ocean mask solutions -- within GEE from the years 2002 to 2020. As the data was visualized within GEE, Ms. Jacobs began to observe specific trends and gain insight into the occurrences of pre-flooding, flooding and the effects after. As her attention shifted to drawing scientific conclusions, Ms. Jacobs began to investigate major flood occurrences over the last two decades, notably several in India. This process consisted of investigating CSR GRACE and GRACE-FO data in Google Earth Engine for specific periods. To conclude her work with GRACE and GRACE-FO, Ms. Jacobs produced a poster and paper outlining several conclusions addressing the effects of elevated water storage in the occurrence of extreme monsoon flooding.
